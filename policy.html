Refund policy

You have 14 days from the receiving date of your order to return products.

Our easy return policy requires two simple steps:

  1. Return the relevant products at your nearest Dejavu store only in the same condition as supplied and in their original packaging with the relevant invoice.

  2. Items purchased online using "Sympl" installments method can only be refunded online and not through our offline stores,  in case of any inquiries please contact our customer service team at: 01157710002

Online Exchange policy 

To exchange an item within the 14 days period, please call our Online Customer Service: 01157710002 or via Facebook or Instagram and inform them what items you need to exchange. Please share all the required details with the customer service representative and you can exchange the item via courier or to your nearest Dejavu store in the same condition as supplied and in their original packaging.


Shipments can not be opened until after the order is delivered and the payment is made. In case of refund/Exchange request, you can contact our online Support Team by calling the following number: 01157710002 or by sending a message through our social media channels regarding the required exchange/return.
Working hours are Sunday to Thursday from 9 am till pm.
